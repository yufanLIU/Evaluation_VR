Once you have read these instructions click on "continue" to start the testing. A screen will be presented.  Click on "Next >>" to show an image.  Then, choose an appropriate quality rating at the bottom of the screen and click on the rating. Click on "Rate" after you have decided a rating. Click on "Next>>" after you have rated an image to display the next image. The count displays the number of images seen by you and the total number of images in the set.
